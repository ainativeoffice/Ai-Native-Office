# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages
- Whitepaper home page (`/`)
- Standalone section and appendix pages (`/sections/<id>/`)
- RFC Log index and blog posts (`/blog/`, `/blog/<slug>/`)
- Signal Log (`/signals/`)
- Public machine-readable discovery files (`robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, `llms.txt`, `llms-full.txt`)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated dashboards
- Admin pages
- Internal API routes that are not public landing content

## Target audience
- Enterprise real estate, infrastructure, compliance, and AI decision-makers evaluating sovereign on-premises AI deployment.

## Primary keywords
- AI-Native Office
- sovereign AI infrastructure
- on-premises AI
- edge AI office
- AI-native commercial real estate
